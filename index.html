Kayo Evetiouz

Brilliantly Injudicious

A man of wit and many talents (and an odd habit of talking in third person, apparently), Kayo Evetiouz is a jack of many trades when it comes to multimedia design. Being an artist is not always easy, especially in a world where they say art is dead, which might just explain why this particular artist is turning to satire and moderately witty commentary in an attempt to catch the attention of potential clients. Well, at least it's working; you're still here, after all. In any case, I think that's enough about me. If people were actually interested in the artist above the art I wouldn't have to turn to satire and fancy formulations to get your attention, right? Right. Below you will find a few fortes of mine, but be not fooled! I may or may not have a few tricks up my sleeve that are yet to be added to the repertoire. If at any point you feel intrigued, I strongly encourage you to contact me via the conveniently placed phone number or email address at the bottom of the page or within the contact page.
